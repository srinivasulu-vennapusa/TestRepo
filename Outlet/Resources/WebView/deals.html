Deals

User can see Current and Past deals. In home page, when Deals icon is tapped, The current deals icon and Past deals icons will get activated.

Sales Executive or the person at the Point of Sale (POS) can see the deals or offers valid on a given day. Store Admin can set up the deals, offers or gift vouchers from time-to-time. It gives the information on both current deals and past deals. The deal can be specific to particular store or across the stores.

Deals, Offers, Gift Vouchers, and Loyalty cards can be configured by using Admin console. The Admin console is responsible for the entire backend operations like Inventory, Creating Product Masters, Warehousing, and other application access and housekeeping operations.

A) Current Deals : Current deals will show the deals valid for today.

B) Past Deals : Past deals will show the deals closed till the last business day.
