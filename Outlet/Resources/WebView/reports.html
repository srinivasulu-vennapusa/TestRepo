Reports

User can see the reports of Sales and Orders.

Reports are required to get the visibility into the business. Reports are only accessible to the management who has permissions. But evaluation copy gives you certain standard reports like Daily Sales. More reports can be accessed by getting commercial License.

A) Sale Reports: Sale reports are to check the reports of daily sales. There will be search criteria to get the details of a particular sale report based on “bill date”, “bill number”, “bill amount”, “paid amount”, “due amount”.

B) Order Reports: Order reports is to check the reports of all orders yet to be executed.. There will be search criteria to get the details of a particular order report based on “order date”, “ order number”, “ order amount”, “order amount”.
