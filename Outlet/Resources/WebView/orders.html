Orders

Omni Retailer has the features to take orders from the field. A sales executive can carry his/her Mobile or Tablet and take the order including card based payment. All this is possible, as long as there is internet connectivity. The Orders can will visible to fulfillment executive immediately. This gives better reach to the customer and orders can be fulfilled much faster. The customer also gets notification on the status of the order and shipment.

The order template and fields can be customized to match the specific business requirement. It has provision to take orders both with payment or without payment. Even partial payment is possible before booking the order. If the system doesn’t find the phone number or email ID, it treats as new customer and the customer details are stored automatically, provided the govt regulations allow the storage of customer details without his/her notice. While taking the payment by using card, it takes the customer signature on the touch screen and the signature also gets stored along with the order.

A) New Orders:
New orders option is to get stock from the inventory when a particular stock is about to finish.

B) Previous Orders: Previous orders are to check the pending and completed orders.
User may search previous orders by “Order ID”, Ordered or delivery date, Ordered or delivery time.
